Responsible Travel

Responsible travel is one of our founding values and governs everything we do. We love
our planet and want to preserve it for future generations, and we know our clients do too.

Carbon Offsetting

When it comes to climate change, our planet needs us to do more to help restore it's natural balance,
which is why we have Patnered with Trees4Travel to offset the CO2 emissions created from our
holidays. Trees4Travel is committed to the long-term success of tree planting to offset CO2. They
select protected sites and indigenous trees that will thrive in each climate, and work with local
communities and organizations to ensure that everyone stands to benefit.

Local Communities

We work closely with all of our suppliers to find and promote activities and experiences run by local enterprises, particularly those that celebrate different communities and cultures. Not only is it important to us that the travel experiences we provide support everyone in the destinations that we offer, it also creates much more authentic and unique experiences for our clients around the world. Whether it’s helping local marine biologists build coral nurseries in Malaysia, witnessing the ‘big 5’ in Kenya with all-female resident ranger teams, or taking part in community art projects in Tulum, your travel specialist will be able to help you find incredible experiences that will make your trip truly special.

Sustainable travel

We work with a wide range of hotels and experience providers who are actively seeking more sustainable practices and we will always highlight those credentials to our clients. From water and waste solutions, to coral reef conservation and innovations in solar energy, many of our partners across the world are leading the way with sustainable practices. Your travel specialist will be able to curate a sustainable trip for you.

Our Promise

Each holiday we create adheres to our strict ethical code on wildlife protection, preservation of the environment and supporting local communities. While we recognise that there is still more work to be done, we are committed to making a positive and meaningful impact on the world, because travel is better when everyone stands to benefit.
